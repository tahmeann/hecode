About Us

Hashi Ekshathe is a dynamic, student-led non-profit dedicated to creating meaningful change in our community. Born from a passion for social impact, we thrive on the energy of young changemakers who drive our mission forward. Through innovative projects and community-focused fundraisers, we tackle pressing issues and stand by those in need during critical times. Our vision is simple yet powerful: to bring people together and make a lasting difference. At Hashi Ekshathe, every initiative is a step toward a brighter, more compassionate future.

Store

Our most dynamic venture is the Hashi Ekshathe Store! Here, students can sell their crafts, keeping the earnings while also having the option to donate a portion if they choose.

This initiative not only provides a platform for student creativity but also helps us secure additional funding for our projects, ensuring we can continue making a positive impact.
